About the Course

Every High School Junior will take the ACT for free on April 8th, 2025. To prepare your student for that exam, I have put together this class that will run from January 26th - April 2nd. Throughout this 10-week class, students will participate in a live, virtual lecture that covers concepts, strategies, and the mindset required to achieve their maximum score on the ACT exam. Each class has a capacity of 10 students, which allows for individual support and full participation. Practice exams and worksheets will be provided for each subject! For a more in-depth breakdown of the course, click here There are three schedule options still available: Sundays 2pm-4pm, Sundays 7pm-9pm, and Wednesdays 7pm-9pm. Please select one that will allow the student to attend most, if not all, of the 10 sessions. Zoom links will be sent upon registration!
